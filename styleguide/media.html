Media

Images

Check out _media.scss for all the styles.

A simple include of an inline image goes thus:

<img src="http://placekitten.com/482/322" alt="A very cute kitten" />

However we now have a wonderful figure tag to wrap media in and give them a caption. If the alt text is a repeat of other text don't include it. (At widescreen setting media is displayed at 50% width).

<figure>
	<img src="http://placekitten.com/482/322" />
	<figcaption>A very cute kitten!</figcaption>
</figure>

You may want to target certain media to make it more of a feature (bigger, flashing borders etc...). Use the .media-feature class.

<figure class="media-feature">
	<img src="http://placekitten.com/482/322" />
	<figcaption>A very cute kitten!</figcaption>
</figure>

Audio

File types

<audio src="http://developer.mozilla.org/@api/deki/files/2926/=AudioTest_(1).ogg" controls>
	Your browser does not support the <code>audio</code> element.
</audio>
<audio src="foo.ogg" controls>
	<track kind="captions" src="foo.en.vtt" srclang="en" label="English">
	<track kind="captions" src="foo.sv.vtt" srclang="sv" label="Svenska">
</audio>
<audio controls="controls">
	Your browser does not support the <code>audio</code> element.
	<source src="foo.wav" type="audio/wav">
</audio>

Video

Here are 2 examples of embedding video. File types

<video src="videofile.ogg" poster="posterimage.jpg" controls>
	Sorry, your browser doesn't support embedded videos,
	but don't worry, you can <a href="videofile.ogg">download it</a>
	and watch it with your favorite video player!
</video>
<video src="foo.ogg" controls="controls">
	<track kind="subtitles" src="foo.en.vtt" srclang="en" label="English">
	<track kind="subtitles" src="foo.sv.vtt" srclang="sv" label="Svenska">
</video>
